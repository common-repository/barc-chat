Barc Chat combines a feature-rich chat room and clean design, encouraging real-time interaction between your visitors. 

== Description ==



### Barc's Benefits:###

- Unlimited Users: Barc lets every voice be heard by not limiting how many people can chat.  
- Pictures & Videos: Your community can bring YouTube, Spotify, Soundcloud and other multimedia directly into the chat.
- Language Translation: Let everyone, regardless of language, chat together in real-time with automatic language translation!
- Private Chats: Give users more privacy with one-on-one chatrooms. 
- Polls: Want an opinion or feedback? Create interactive polls directly in the chat. 
- Emojis: Let users express themselves with hundreds of eye-catching emojis.
- Spam Protection: Say no to spam bots! Barc's built in spam protector makes sure your chat stays secure.
- Word Filter: Keep your chat clean by filtering adult language.
- Email Alerts: Make sure you're always part of the conversion by getting email alerts on the rooms you care about.
- Styling: Want more creative control? Customize the the look and feel of your Barc chat room.
- Moderation: Website owners can unlock moderation control which provides features such as Deleting comments, Banning users, Clearing the chat or Disabling guests from chatting.
- Voice/Video Calling: Bring your conversations to life with video conferencing in crystal clear quality with up to six other people - without needing to download any software! Currently available for Chrome and Firefox.


A compelling social solution for any site, Barc is extremely fast and simple to install. Chat with us on Barc.com or Download the plugin to get the conversation started!

== Installation ==

Barc takes less than a minute to setup.

- To begin click on the download button, and your download will begin. From there go to your wordpress administration page. Click on Plugins, then click on add new, and select ‘upload.’ Drag the Barc file into the choose upload button - and simply click on install now. Once the installation completes activate the plugin.
